Our story

A Retreat on Portugal's Silver Coast

Nestled along one of Europe's most unspoiled coastlines, Silverside Holidays sits at Boavista, between São Martinho do Porto and Foz d'Arelho — a privileged spot on Portugal's Silver Coast, 70 km north of Lisbon.

Whether you choose one of our spacious suites or a fully-equipped apartment, every room features a private terrace, TV and Wi-Fi with stunning ocean views. The property also offers a swimming pool, breakfast room, electric vehicle charging and a large garden.

  • Private terrace & ocean view in every suite
  • Swimming pool & landscaped garden
  • Breakfast served daily
  • TV, Wi-Fi, EV charging & free parking
  • Licensed tourism — RNET n.º 8734

Activities Nearby

Between São Martinho do Porto and Foz d'Arelho, the Silver Coast offers wild beaches, historic towns and endless outdoor adventures.

Surfing

World-class Atlantic swells just minutes away. Lessons available for all levels.

Hiking

Coastal trails through cliffs, pine forests and protected nature reserves.

Óbidos & Culture

The medieval walled town of Óbidos is a 20-minute drive away.

Cycling

Bike rentals available. Scenic routes along the coast and inland hills.

Fishing

Traditional Atlantic fishing from the rocks or join a local boat trip.

Beaches

Pristine Silver Coast beaches within minutes — São Martinho do Porto and Foz d'Arelho lagoon.

Local Gastronomy

Fresh seafood, grilled fish and local wines. The best of Portuguese cuisine.

Alcobaça & Batalha

UNESCO World Heritage monasteries just a short drive from Silverside.
